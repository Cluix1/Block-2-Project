This demo simulates a vulnerable login in Python. Try a normal login, then try the injection string.

Demo Instructions

Password: s3cret

Expected: Regular login succeeded.

Step 2: Injection Attempt

Username: admin' OR '1'='1

Password: anything

Expected: Injection succeeded (bypassed login).
